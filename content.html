01. About Moon

In practice, Moon is an ecosystem that allows people to attend to local events right now or in the next days. By ecosystem, we mean integration with known platforms (Slack, Microsoft teams, ...), a newsletter and a mobile application. By integrating a social media side (sharing the events you go to via Instagram stories, via Tiktok), we plan to leverage the social network and the huge active community on them

On the other side, Moon is also a platform for professionals to promote their events, accessing an active community in 3 clicks and checking the analytics of their events or even events trends in Paris (where do people hang out the most, what do they do, ...)
